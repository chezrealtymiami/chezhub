Welcome to Chez Realty 🧡

This is your onboarding guide. Follow each step in order to get fully activated and ready to close your first deal.

Block 1 — Getting Started
Set up your Chez identity before anything else.
1
📬 Set up your @chezre.com email
Check your personal email for a Google Workspace invite. Accept it, create your password, log in at mail.google.com and enable 2-factor authentication.
2
📧 Read your Chez welcome email
We sent a welcome email to your Chez address with photographer info, what to wear, and your first steps. Read it carefully.
3
📸 Schedule your professional headshot
Contact Krastina Dimitrova — Firefly By The Sea · (617) 794-2903 · 13020 SW 120th St, Miami FL. Wear soft neutral colors — black, navy, or light tones.
Block 2 — Activation
Complete your admin steps to get fully activated.
⚠️
Step 4 is mandatory. Without completing the Side Thursday training, we cannot move forward with your onboarding.
4
🎓 Register for mandatory Side Thursday training
Register at side.360learning.com as soon as your Side systems are active. This is required before moving to the next block.
5
📋 Fill out your agent information form
Send your full name, email, phone, birthday, shirt size, license number and home address to admin@chezre.com.
6
🏛️ Apply for Miami Association of Realtors
For new agents only. Choose the REALTOR membership, pay in full or 4 installments, and also apply for SUPRA EKey. Skip if already a Realtor.
Block 3 — Your Tech Stack
The tools you'll use every day to manage leads and close deals.
7
🔑 Follow Up Boss (FUB) — your CRM
Complete FUB bootcamp, connect your Chez email, and watch the Smart Lists and Handraiser Alerts videos on Chez University.
8
🏘 Matrix MLS — 5 training videos
Watch all 5 Matrix Best Practices videos on Chez University (Vol. 1 through Vol. 5).
9
🏡 Zillow — profile & Flex agreement
Create your Zillow agent profile, message Admin for the team invite, sign the Flex Agreement at zillow.com/pbflex/agent, and complete Zillow Academy.
10
🌐 Realtor.com — accept team invite
We'll send you the team invite when you reach this step. Accept it and complete your profile with headshot, bio, and license number.
11
⭐ Ylopo — setup & training
Wait for Admin to send the Ylopo training email before starting. We fill out the questionnaire for you.
Block 4 — Ready to Sell
Final steps before your first deal.
12
📝 Review scripts — focus on Zillow ALM
Study the Chez scripts. The Zillow ALM script is your primary lead conversion tool — read it, practice out loud, and know it cold before your 1-on-1.
13
🤝 1-on-1 with Jose & Dexter
Your alignment call with the founders — goals, strategy, and your first 90 days at Chez. Contact Admin to schedule it.
