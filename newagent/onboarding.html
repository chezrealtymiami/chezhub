Why this matters
This is one of the most important steps in your onboarding. Creating a dedicated Chrome profile with your @chezre.com account ensures that ALL your Chez tools, logins, and accounts stay organized under one professional identity — separate from your personal life.

2️⃣ Click your profile icon (top right corner)

5️⃣ Name this profile "Chez Realty"
6️⃣ From now on, use this Chrome profile for every Chez tool and platform

ALM Script — Zillow Live Connection
Study both scripts below and practice out loud. Use the AI mock call tool to prepare for your 1-on-1 role play session with Jose & Dexter.
📞 ALM: Zillow Live Connection

Hi (customer name)! This is (your name). I'm your local agent in Miami. I see you're interested in 123 Main Street. When would you like to see it?

What do you like about this particular home? (or these homes if they mentioned a few)

DIG IN — GET THEM TO SPEAK ABOUT THEIR NEEDS, WANTS & DESIRES

Did any other properties catch your attention that you wanted to see?

We obviously got connected through Zillow. How else have you been looking at homes?

(Customer Name) IT WAS A PLEASURE TALKING TO YOU & I LOOK FORWARD TO HELPING YOU. I WILL ALSO SEND YOU A TEXT WITH MY DIGITAL BUSINESS CARD THAT HAS ALL MY CONTACT INFORMATION. AGAIN MY NAME IS ________ AND MY EMAIL IS _________.

HAVE A GREAT DAY!
Rebuttal Script — Objection Handling
💬 Rebuttals to Objections
I WANT TO WORK THE LISTING AGENT…
— OKAY & WHY IS THAT?
I DON'T TRUST REAL ESTATE AGENTS…
— HONESTLY I DON'T TRUST A LOT OF THEM EITHER BUT WHAT MAKES YOU SAY THAT?
NOT READY TO WORK WITH AN AGENT…
— WHEN YOU SAY WORK WITH AN AGENT, WHAT DO YOU MEAN?
— WHEN WILL YOU BE READY TO WORK WITH AN AGENT?
BECAUSE THEN I CAN SAVE MONEY!
— ACTUALLY, THE LISTING AGENT JUST RECEIVES A HIGHER COMMISSION AND REMEMBER THEY HAVE A FIDUCIARY RESPONSIBILITY TO THEIR CLIENTS WHICH ARE THE SELLERS. I WOULD BE YOUR AGENT MAKING SURE YOU GET THE BEST DEAL AND NEGOTIATING ON YOUR BEHALF FROM START TO FINISH.
I AM WAITING A YEAR OR SO…
— IF THE IDEAL HOME CAME ON THE MARKET IN THE NEXT MONTH OR TWO WOULD YOU BE OPEN TO SEEING IT?
— What's the advantage of buying next year?
NOT SURE I WANT TO MOVE…
— Whenever you decide to move, what are you hoping to change from your living situation now?
I WANT TO RENT…
— ARE YOU PLANNING ON LEAVING THE AREA IN A FEW YEARS?
— WHAT IS THE ADVANTAGE OF RENTING FOR ANOTHER YEAR?
— IS THERE A BENEFIT OF YOU RENTING FOR ANOTHER YEAR?
I'M JUST BROWSING…
— THAT'S FINE, MOST OF MY CLIENTS ARE JUST BROWSING. TELL ME MORE ABOUT WHAT YOU'RE BROWSING?
DON'T HAVE MONEY FOR A DOWNPAYMENT…
— HOW MUCH MONEY DO YOU THINK YOU NEED FOR A DOWNPAYMENT?
WHAT'S YOUR COMMISSION? WHAT DO YOU CHARGE?
Seller:
— Are you just curious about the total cost of selling? We will definitely cover that.
— Cost is obviously important. What else is important about the agent you use to sell your home?
— Is commission the only thing you're looking at when picking an agent?

YES… My commission is a range from 4-7% depending on the marketing needed, but we will go over all of that when we meet.
NO… What are the other deciding factors?
Buyer:
— Lucky for you the seller pays all of the fees!
